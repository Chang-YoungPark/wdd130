Main Historic Place

The tower of Bulgangsa

It is The Gate of Non-duality, the gage of Geonbongsa Temple that was not lost during the Korean War.

In 1666, Song-Si-yeol built Amseojae on the rocks, where he studied and taught students. This is the center of Hwayang-gugok and the pond was named Geumsa-dam because the sand seen through the clear water appears like gold dust.
